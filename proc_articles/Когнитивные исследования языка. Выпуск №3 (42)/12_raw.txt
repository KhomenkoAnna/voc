Within the cognitive approach to the analysis of language structures the article addresses some issues of cross-category interaction in the processes of secondary conceptualization of knowledge about space in language. Metaphorical representa-tions, the sources of which are English natural objects, serve as a cognitive basis of correlation between basic categories Key words: cross-category, secondary conceptualization, space, metaphorical representations.

The paper discusses the cognitive aspects of the poetic picture of the world. It is an attempt to elaborate the term and define the main perspectives of further research. The poetic picture of the world reflects an individual emotional experience and stages in the development of the personal consciousness. So it must be studied as a separate object.
Key words: poetic worldview, poetic linguistic personality, poetic discourse, poetic text. (Белгород, Россия) Белгородский государственный национальный исследовательский университет
